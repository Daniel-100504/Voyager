      04 · About

      About Voyager

      Voyager is a lightweight travel-weather companion built for Version 1 scope: dashboard, forecast, search, advisory, and alerts.

      How it works

      • Weather dashboard — live temperature, condition, humidity, and wind for your selected location.
      • 7-day forecast — daily highs, lows, and conditions, plus a 24-hour temperature trend.
      • Location search — find any city or municipality worldwide.
      • Travel advisory — an automated 🟢🟡🔴 rating based on today's conditions.
      • Weather alerts — a banner appears site-wide when severe weather is detected.

      Data sources

      Forecast data is provided by meteoblue. Location search uses the free Open-Meteo geocoding service.

      Travel advisories are generated automatically from weather data and are a general guide only — always confirm conditions with official local authorities before traveling.
